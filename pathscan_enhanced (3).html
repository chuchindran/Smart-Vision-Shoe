Runs entirely in your browser — no video is uploaded or stored.
Distance in meters is estimated from where an obstacle's edge appears in the frame, combined with your mount height, tilt, and field-of-view settings — not a true depth sensor. Accuracy depends on calibrating those settings to how the camera is actually worn.
Object labels come from a general-purpose recognition model (~80 common object types) and require an internet connection the first time to download — after that it runs locally. Many real hazards (curbs, steps, poles, uneven ground) won't be named but are still caught by the edge/distance detector above.
